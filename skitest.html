First page back in 2004

June 2023 Sandrina

I always wanted to design a website and in 2004 I was approached by a family member to create a business website. Of course I agreed and with the help of a HTML book I got started. Without any coding experience I had to use FrontPage. Luckily, that was only once. I still like the look of the site. It's a simple, clean and well-structured design offering all necessary information.

The business owner has always done the maintenance and content updates ever since the site was created. I've suggested a redesign a few times but some people don't like change. Such an old page isn't responsive and doesn't follow accessibility principles, I am aware of that. That is why I won't stop suggesting...
