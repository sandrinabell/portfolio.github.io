My web journey

The story so far

June 2023 Sandrina

In 2004, a family member needed a website. Great, I do it! Although working as an IT specialist I didn't have any HTML knowledge and used Frontpage. Yes, shame on me. That page is still online. I'd love to redesign the page but visitors appreciate that there is a website which isn't undergoing constant design changes and so my first 'work of art' looks really ancient by today's standards. But I was hooked - big time. I love the combination of technical and design principles to create functional, visually appealing and user-friendly websites

Despite my new passion, it has taken me 20 years to create this page. I'd still develop websites for friends but kept working as system and network administrator. After starting a family, I decided to qualify as teacher which allowed me to teach IT during school hours. I enjoy helping people to improve their IT skills, to increase their employability and to show them how to stay safe online.

Luckily, my web passion came out of hibernation when a friend asked if I could take over the web development of her company's web appearance in 2018 using WordPress. I was back in the game and still am now. A successful redesign (including hosting, platform and theme change) put me back on the right track and started to study web development at the Open University in 2019 and completed my DipHE in May 2023. Equipped with my new knowledge, it's full steam ahead now! Creating my GitHub portfolio as well as coding loads of HTML CSS JavaScript and PHP projects is keeping me happily busy. Back to uni in October to complete my BSc in web development.
