beatrix-travel.com

WordPress web dev since 2018

June 2023 Sandrina

Beatrix Travel's managing director was unhappy with the website produced by a local agency and asked me to take over. It's a WordPress design and had a major redesign in 2022 due to necessary work (web hosting move and a theme migration). An online marketing agency commented on the design looking like a blog rather than a travel website. I agree but the owner likes it that way. The customer is king.
